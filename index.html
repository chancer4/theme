The Pursuit of Development

In October I began my journey to becoming a developer by enrolling in a coding bootcamp called DigitalCrafts. The weeks leading up to beginning of class I really began to think about why I wanted to delve into the growing tech industry. Besides always having an interest in computers and the internet and recognizing the potential for growth in the industry, I was interested in the "behind the scenes" aspect of coding. I enjoy knowing the backstory. I like learning about the process that got something to the current version and the steps it took to achieve it. I thought that web development would be a great path to set out on to achieve this goal of getting a glimse of the backstage of the internet.
